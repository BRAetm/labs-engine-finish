The Labs Launcher needs a plugin-based capture system like Helios. Currently it only has MfCaptureSource (Media Foundation). Add support for:

## Required Capture Plugins

1. **OBSCapture** - Connect to OBS via WebSocket (fastest, GPU-accelerated)
2. **DSCapture** - DirectShow capture
3. **MagewellCapture** - Magewell capture card
